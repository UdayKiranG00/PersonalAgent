# PersonalAgent

A personal agent to manage mails.

Peforms following operations:
1. send mails.
2. summarises mails.
3. can perform filter search.
4. trash automation (can move mails to trash by specifying certain categories eg: Promotional, Advertisement).
5. saves mail addresses and contact name (useful when specifying people by names).

# I asked it "What are your capabilities, explain?"
# Response:
I am your Technical Gmail Manager, designed to help you organize your inbox, manage your contacts, track tasks, and perform system-level operations. Here is a detailed explanation of my capabilities:
#### 📧 Gmail Management
I can handle your email workflow to keep your inbox clean and professional:
*   **Email Retrieval & Analysis:** I can list your unread messages and read the full details (sender, subject, and body) of specific emails to provide summaries or categorizations.
*   **Sending Emails:** I can compose and send professional HTML emails. I can look up recipient addresses from your contacts list if they aren't provided.
*   **Inbox Cleanup:**
    *   **Manual Deletion:** I can move specific emails to the trash (always after confirming with you).
    *   **Trash Automation:** I can trigger automated processes to clear out unwanted or useless emails.

#### 👥 Contact Management
I help you maintain an organized address book:
*   **Contact Retrieval:** I can fetch your existing list of contacts.
*   **Saving Contacts:** Whenever I encounter a new email address in your correspondence, I can save it as a new contact for you.

#### 📋 Task Planning & Tracking
For complex requests, I act as a project manager:
*   **Task Breakdown:** If a goal requires multiple steps, I break it down into sub-tasks.
*   **Tracking:** I use a task management system to `append`, `update`, and `delete` tasks, ensuring nothing is missed and providing a clear status (pending, done, cancelled, or failed) for each step.

#### 💻 System Operations
I have the ability to interact with the local environment:
*   **Command Execution:** I can execute Windows command prompt commands to create folders, manage files, and read/write data in the current directory.

### 🧠 How I Work (My Logic)
*   **Scratchpad:** I maintain an internal "scratchpad" to track my reasoning and strategy, ensuring my actions align with your goals.
*   **Professionalism:** All outgoing communications are written professionally, starting with a greeting and ending with a standard signature.
*   **Safety First:** I never assume; if I'm missing information or about to delete data, I will stop and ask for your confirmation.
